Cosmopolitan

A vibrant, lipsmackingly sweet and sour party in a glass? Yes, its a Cosmopolitan!

Ingredients:

  • 35ml vodka
  • 15ml orange liqueur
  • 25ml cranberry juice
  • squeeze of fresh lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • orange peel twist, for garnish

Method:

  1. Fill cocktail shaker 3/4 full with ice cubes.
  2. Add vodka, orange liqueur, cranberry juice and fresh lime into shaker.
  3. Shake vigorously for ten seconds until cold.
  4. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with the orange peel twist.
