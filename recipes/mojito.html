Mojito

An irresistibly refreshing one way salsa dance to the Caribbean!

Ingredients:

  • 50ml rum
  • 20ml fresh lime juice
  • handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • crushed ice
  • fresh mint sprig to garnish

Method:

  1. Pour the lime juice into a glass, add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  2. Add all the mint leaves into the glass.
  3. Add the crushed ice.
  4. Using the spoon, stir the ice with the mint leaves until the mint starts to break.
  5. Add the rum and stir until evenly mixed.
  6. Add the sprig of mint to garnish.
