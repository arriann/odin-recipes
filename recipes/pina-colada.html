Pina Colada

Do you like Pina Coladas? What about getting caught in the rain?

Ingredients:

  • 50ml white rum
  • 50ml pineapple juice
  • 50ml coconut cream
  • squeeze of fresh lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • wedge of pineapple for garnish

Method:

  1. Add white rum, pineapple juice, coconut cream, fresh lime and ice to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass and garnish with a wedge of pineapple.
